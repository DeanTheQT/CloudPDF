What CloudPDF Does

Registered users can upload thesis PDFs, validate whether a document is a thesis, generate AI-powered summaries, extract highlights, keywords, and APA citations, and review previous uploads in their history.

Who Can Use The Summarizer

The summarizing tools are only available to registered users with an active account. Visitors can browse this page, create an account, or sign in to access the summarizer and account features.

For Students and Researchers

The platform is designed for research organization, especially for thesis and academic manuscript workflows. Admin tools also support user management, messaging, logs, and archived-item recovery.

Admin Contact

If you need help with your account or system access, contact the admin team through the in-app messaging feature after logging in, or reach out through support@CloudPDF.local.

Core Features

Thesis Validation

Checks whether uploaded PDFs match the expected academic thesis format before processing.

AI Summary Generation

Creates summaries with optional highlights, keywords, and APA citation output.

History and Review

Keeps a searchable record of generated summaries for logged-in users.

Admin Assistance

Supports user-to-admin messaging and admin replies for help, moderation, and support.
